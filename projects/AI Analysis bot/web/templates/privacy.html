Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-17

What we collect

When you create an account, we store your email address and a cryptographic hash of your password. When you use the analysis features, we store the symbol, timeframe, and timestamp of each analysis request so we can render your history page.

What we don't collect

We do not sell your data. We do not run third-party tracking pixels. We do not log your IP address beyond what's needed to keep the service secure.

How we protect your data

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt before storage. Session cookies are signed with a server-side secret and transmitted only over HTTPS in production. The database is hosted on Neon (serverless Postgres in Singapore).

Your rights

You can delete your account at any time. Email privacy@1percentailab.com and we'll remove your row within 30 days.
